OUR WORK
SPEAK FOR ITSELF

ETIENNE is a unique collection of premium furniture masterpieces rooted in international design and craft traditions. Spanning generations and continents, exclusive designs from the 1950s to the present day share harmonious proportions and a simple sensibility.

OURSTORY

CREATED BASED ON THE
CONCEPT OF EXCLUSIVITY

Our furniture is imagined and designed to be part of your life for years to come.

Étienne features timeless furniture, with natural fabrics, curved lines, plenty of mirrors and classic design, which can be incorporated into any decor project. The pieces enchant for their sobriety, to last for generations, faithful to the shapes of each period, with a touch of the present.

The pieces stand out for their contemporary straight lines and imposing presence. Current, following the world trend of the great masters, the furniture stands out for its noble and innovative materials, composing sophisticated and exclusive environments.

VISIT OUR STUDIO

We believe that good design tells a story.

Studio Étienne is a full-service interior design firm that designs homes from concept to completion all over the France. We have garnered a reputation for beautifully balanced spaces that feel refreshingly modern and endlessly classic. We create custom interiors for full new builds and renovations that stand the test of time.
